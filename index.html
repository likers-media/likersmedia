About

Rooted in Africa.
Built to scale.

Likers Media is an integrated marketing company incorporated in Cameroon and headquartered in Akwa, Douala. Our core mission is to promote localism — building visibility, infrastructure and market presence for African brands that deserve a bigger stage.

We operate across the full marketing spectrum: experiential campaigns, trade fairs, white-label brand solutions, digital automation systems and flagship programmes that have become landmarks in the Cameroonian marketing calendar.

As a Meta-verified business partner, we also operate enterprise-grade digital communication infrastructure — including WhatsApp Business API — for clients across banking, hospitality, FMCG and regulated industries across Central Africa.
